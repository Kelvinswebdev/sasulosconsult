Fully Licensed & Internationally Compliant Recruitment Agency

Sasulos Consult Limited operates in full compliance with Ghanaian labor regulations and international recruitment standards. We are a legally registered recruitment firm authorized to facilitate ethical overseas employment and manpower supply. Our processes align with host country employment laws, ensuring transparent documentation, verified candidate screening, and lawful deployment procedures.

We implement:

  • Rigorous candidate screening and background verification: We ensure all recruitment activities adhere to Ghanaian labor laws and the legal requirements of the destination country, including work permits, visas, and employment contracts.
  • Structured documentation and visa processing coordination: Our team meticulously manages all candidate documentation, ensuring accuracy and compliance with international standards to facilitate smooth deployment.
  • Clear contractual agreements between all parties: We prioritize ethical recruitment practices, sourcing candidates through transparent channels and providing them with clear information about job roles, compensation, and working conditions.
  • Ongoing communication and post-deployment support Our structured processes ensure that every stage of recruitment—from candidate screening to deployment—is executed with precision and reliability, minimizing risks for employers.
